About

Meet Nick Verzilli

Based in Pittsburgh with 25+ years shaping stories for film, television, and branded content, Nick thrives on collaboration first - understanding your vision and making it better together. His technical expertise in workflow optimization and hardware solutions keeps projects running smoothly, while his instinct for pacing and emotional storytelling ensures every cut serves the narrative.

Trained through internships with editors and directors on films you've seen, Nick has collaborated remotely with teams worldwide, offering everything from supervised edit sessions to complete post-production management. When existing tools fall short, he builds solutions like VAMP (Video Asset Management Platform) and an AI-powered multicam plugin for Premiere, born from real editor frustrations and daily workflow needs.

While his portfolio spans commercials, corporate content, and social media campaigns, Nick's heart lies in narrative work - both long and short-form storytelling where character and emotion drive every edit.
